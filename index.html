About Thomas Woodham-Smith

With over two decades of expertise in antiques and decorative arts, Thomas Woodham-Smith has established himself as a leading authority in fine furniture and design. His carefully curated collection spans centuries of craftsmanship, from Georgian mahogany to Arts and Crafts masterpieces.

Each piece in our collection has been selected for its exceptional quality, historical significance, and enduring beauty. We believe in the value of timeless design and the stories objects tell.
